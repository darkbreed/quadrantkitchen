Who is this guy?

Well, my name is Ben Reed and this is my little corner of the internet for all things food and drink related. Day to day I am a mobile app developer and architect, but in the evenings and at weekends my wife and I enjoy all things food related, cooking, brewing, curing, smoking & preserving to name but a few. I also enjoy food photography, but I am very much an amateur when it comes to this!

Here you will find recipes that I've noted down along the way, blogs about homebrewing and charcuterie amongst many other things, and reviews of various places we have been on our travels.
